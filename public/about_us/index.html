About Us



We are a full service Grooming Salon found in East Brunswick in 1994 by Carol, an avid pet lover and graduate of Nash Academy. Carol has been grooming for 30 years and as a young girl graduated her first school from Poodle Grooming School of America in North Jersey. She is very experienced and has worked in many shops during her career.

Inna has been grooming for 10 years and is also graduate of the Nash Academy. A very talented and caring groomer her speciality is all dogs and cats.

Our ongoing mission is to manifest our love of pets into a clean and professionally run Pet Salon.

We achieve this by hiring only experienced people who deeply care for Pets. Knowing and implementing the technical aspects of our trade, and respecting and appreciating our customers.

We are located at 737 Rt. 18 South Side (between Racetrack and Brunswick Square Mall), East Brunswick, NJ

Feel free to stop by and check us out and make an appointment.
